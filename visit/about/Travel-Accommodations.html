      • Local Airport

        John Wayne | (SNA)

        18601 Airport WaySanta Ana, CA 92707
        18601 Airport WaySanta Ana, CA 92707

        Tip

        It's a short drive from SNA to Saddleback Church, and because Disneyland is nearby, it stays fairly competitive in price.

      • Other Airports

        Los Angeles | (LAX)

        1 World WayLos Angeles, CA 90045
        1 World WayLos Angeles, CA 90045

        Tip

        LAX is more traffic intensive, both at the airport and on the freeways to the church. If using LAX, plan on a 1-2 hour commute to Saddleback Church, depending on your arrival time and traffic volume.

      • Ontario | (ONT)12900 E Airport Dr Ste 1245Ontario, CA 91761
        12900 E Airport Dr Ste 1245Ontario, CA 91761

        Tip

        ONT is less traffic intensive both at the airport and on the freeways. A 1 hour commute time is generally all it should take from ONT to Saddleback Church, when using the Toll Roads. It may take more time if you only travel on the freeway.
